THE CONCEPT

A shield you can read — and a truth you can verify

The mark fuses two ideas at the heart of Human Insurance: the shield of protection, and the check of honest, verified truth. It says what the brand says — we explain what's covered, and what's not, before you decide.

The shield = protection

Unmistakably insurance — but soft and rounded, never corporate or aggressive.

The check = verified truth

In a calm gold — the one accent that signals trust, clarity, and "this has been checked for you."

Speaks human

The rounded form reads almost like a chat bubble — insurance, translated into human language.

CONSTRUCTION

Clear space & minimum size

Keep clear space of at least the height of the check around the mark. The mark stays legible down to 16 px; below that, use the simplified single-colour version.

CONCEPT II · ZEN ENSŌ

A Zen circle — drawn in one breath, left open

Inspired by your reference. The ensō is a hand-brushed Zen circle — in Japanese practice an open ensō means nothing is hidden and perfection isn't pretended. A confident check rises through the opening: honest, human, verified. It's the Human Insurance philosophy in one stroke.
